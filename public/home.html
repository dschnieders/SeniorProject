PEC Health and Wellness Program

PEC's health and wellness program provides whole person-centered care and services, illness prevention and health promotion, co-curricular learning opportunities, and health leadership and expterise. All are tailored to support our employees' health, wellbeing, and personal development, the health of the company community and the mission of PEC.

Plans built to fit your lifestyle.

With the Health and Wellness program, employees are able to create a unique plan that fit's their needs and schedule. No more worrying about paying for an expensive personal trainer or trying a new diet on your own; a certified professional is available in house for your health and wellness needs.

Health

The goal of the program is overall health - financial, mental, and physical.

Dietary

Assistance with dietary planning is availble. Informal presentations for recipe ideas and diet changes occur bimonthly.

Fitness

Exercise plans tailored to each individuals needs can be created following a brief one-on-one meeting with our Health and Wellness Coordinator.

Some success stories
